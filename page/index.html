Animals Types


Animals are known as multicellular organisms belonging to the
animal kingdom. It differs from plants by several distinctive
characteristics, such as its ability to move, not metabolize by
photosynthesis, its response to stimuli, its limited growth, and the
stability of its physical structure. Most animals reproduce sexually
and adult cells have two copies of the genetic material. Animal cells
have organic organs like all levels of cells, each serving a specific
function.
